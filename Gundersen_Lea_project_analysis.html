Site Intent:

Where to begin when it comes to re-designing OCCS? This website is a mess in every aspect. My goal for this website is to start with consolidating it's 18 pages into 6, laconic and insightful pages. These 6 pages will be named, Home, Cleaning, Washing, Specials, Appointments, and Equipment. Also, I plan on changing the whole face of the website. It is lacking in aesthetics, organization and interest. Further, the HTML and CSS files have a numerous amount of errors, these files will be updated to HTML5 and CSS3 elements. Lastly, I will arrange and correct the OCCS's information to make it deliberately for its targeted audience. This will make it easy to find and understand, which will satisfy the user. In the end, OCCS will be a desirable and informative website.
